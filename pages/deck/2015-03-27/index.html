Evaluation

  • How good the topics are
    • Coherency
    • Consistency
    • Distinctiveness


Still an open question, do not put too much pressure on yourself

Limitations

  • Does not model the overlapping/correlated topics
  • Word order does not matter
  • Not robust to noise or common words.
    • Needs a good stop word list curated for your dataset
    • You need to remove very rare words and very frequent ones
    • Make sure you run a stemmer or lemmatizer before doing any topic modeling
